About The Musketeers

History

The Musketeers of the Guard were a military branch under the French Monarchies royal household, known as the Maison du Roi. They were tasked with defending the king outside of his residence, and were armed with muskets.

The Musketeers however faced a rough history. Repeatedly disbanded and formed by the kings of the time, up until 1816 when the company was permanently shutdown.

After several centuries, descendants of the Musketeers decided to study their ancestors practices and regroup with a new mission.

Manifesto

The Musketeers after reflecting there history decided, we are not here to defend any king. Rather, we are here to hunt the kings of the universe.

We methodically locate and track the capital ships of the people who treat the universe as their chessboard, and before they even have a chance to ignite their engines, we take them down.

We’re not in this for profit or rank, the Musketeers fight this battle for it’s purpose.

Creed

Our creed is not an extensive list of rules, it merely represents our core value.

All for one, and one for all.

We are not just an organization, we are a brotherhood. We do not concern ourselves with rank, but we pride ourselves on having respect for one another.

What is Star Citizen?

From the mind of Chris Roberts, acclaimed creator of Wing Commander and Freelancer, comes STAR CITIZEN. 100% crowd funded, Star Citizen aims to create a living, breathing science fiction universe with unparalleled immersion… and you’re invited to follow every step of development!

More than a space combat sim, more than a first person shooter and more than an MMO: Star Citizen is the First Person Universe that will allow for unlimited gameplay.
